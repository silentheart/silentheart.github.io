Cardinal Kings: Guardians of the Cardinal Rosario

January 2016 - Present

This was a group project from CGT 445: Video Game Design and Development at Purdue University. You play as the next ruler of the village who needs to prove he or she is worthy of ruling by traveling the four cardinal directions and gathering essences from the Guardians of each direction. North is the Black Turtle, east is the Azure Dragon, South is the Vermillion Bird, and west is the White Tiger. Created in Unreal Engine 4.
